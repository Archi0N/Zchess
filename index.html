Local duel or sparring AI

Zchess

A distilled chess duel on an 8x8 board. Face another player or train against a static Black AI without leaving the page.

Mode
Turn White to move
Status White moves first.

Rules

  • White moves first. In Play vs AI, you control White and Black replies automatically.
  • Click one of your pieces, then a highlighted destination.
  • Captures happen by moving onto an enemy piece's square.
  • Pieces now use chess movement: King, Queen, Rook, Bishop, Knight, and Pawn.
  • There is no castling, en passant, promotion, or check/checkmate system yet.
  • Capture the enemy King to finish the game.
  • When the game ends, it displays GAME OVER and the winner: White win or Black win.
  • Use Pause to freeze the board, Continue to resume, Save to store progress, and Load to continue later.

Piece Guide

♔

King

Moves 1 square in any direction. If it falls, the match ends.

♕

Queen

Slides any number of squares horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

♖

Rook

Slides any number of squares horizontally or vertically.

♗

Bishop

Slides any number of squares diagonally.

♘

Knight

Jumps in an L shape: 2 in one direction, then 1 perpendicular.

♙

Pawn

Moves forward 1, may advance 2 from its starting row, and captures diagonally.
